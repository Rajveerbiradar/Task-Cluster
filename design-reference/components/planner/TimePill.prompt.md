The signature time pill — the app's one piece of drama; use to show a task's remaining time.

```jsx
<TimePill status="on-track" label="2h 40m" />
<TimePill status="overdue" label="−2h 14m" />
```

status: calm (renders nothing) | on-track (blue) | close (amber) | due (red) | overdue (red capsule, counting up). The overdue capsule is the loudest the UI ever gets.
